About Us – Butterfly Calling

Butterfly Calling was born from a vision of blending electronic music with a spiritual experience rooted in nature — a space where music is not only heard but deeply felt.

Inspired by nature, butterflies, and collective consciousness, each of our gatherings is more than just a festival. It is a space of transformation, self-exploration, and a call from heart to heart.

Our goal is to place music at the center of our connection, while creating authentic bonds among participants and offering a timeless experience.

Butterfly Calling is not just an event brand — it’s a community. A place where we dance, share, and grow together.

We invite everyone who resonates with this spirit to join us on the journey.
